FAQ

Why can't I download anymore from the MEGA mirror on the Umineko Project Website?

For free users, MEGA limits the amount you can download in 6 hours. Please retry the download in once more than 6 hours have passed. Also, see the MEGA FAQ "What is transfer quota on MEGA?".

Are 07th-mod and Umineko Project related?

No, they are separate modding groups. While we have communicated in the past, generally we are completely separate modding groups working on completely different types of mods for Umineko.

Historical Context

07th-mod used to host a mirror of the Umineko Project. However, around 13/07/2024 we shutdown our main download server, and no longer host the Umineko Project downloads.
